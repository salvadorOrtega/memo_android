What is Dual-N-Back?
N-Back is a working memory exercise where you have to remember the previous N items and signal when the next one is a repeat. Dual-N-Back takes the task a step further by having you remember two different kinds of inputs, one auditory, one visual, at the same time. It is the only mental workout game with any credible research showing that practicing and getting better at it will make you better at other cognitive skills unrelated to the game itself.

How to play
In the settings, choose your n-back level of difficulty and how many turns per game you want. Let's say you go with level 2, or 2-back. Go to the game page and press the square button to begin; a 3x3 grid will appear.

You'll see a square on the grid activate by turning red and hear a letter sound on each turn. Press the left button (or left arrow-key on your keyboard) if the square activated is the same one that got activated 2 turns ago. Press the right button (or the right arrow-key on your keyboard) if the sound you heard is the same one you heard 2 turns ago.

If you're playing n-back level 3, or 3-back, press the left button if the square activated is the same one that got activated 3 turns ago, and similarly for sound by pressing the right button. Activating 'Immediate Feedback' in the settings will cause the game to give you instant response every time you press the buttons at each turn, showing you a green shadow if you got it right and a red one if you didn't.
